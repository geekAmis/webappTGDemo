About

The Golden Vein lottery is a weekly draw where participants have the chance to win a share of the prize pool. The more tickets purchased, the higher the prize, and a portion of the proceeds goes to support local community projects.

How to Play

1. Sign up for an account on the Golden Vein website.
2. Deposit funds into your account using one of the supported payment methods.
3. Select the number of tickets you wish to purchase for the upcoming draw.
4. Cross your fingers and wait for the draw to see if you've won!

Prizes

The prize pool for each draw varies based on the number of participants and tickets sold. Prizes are distributed among winners based on the number of tickets they hold, with larger prizes awarded to those with more tickets.
